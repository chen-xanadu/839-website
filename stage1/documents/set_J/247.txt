<p>Jonas</p> <p>Candide</p>, a former carnival showman, travels around the South in 1918 with his own portable electric chair, going from prison to prison with his young assistant, <p>Jimmy</p>, charging one hundred dollars per execution. Two of <p>Jonas</p>' potential victims are siblings <p>Willy</p> and <p>Gundred</p> <p>Herzallerliebst</p>. While <p>Jonas</p> successfully executes <p>Willy</p>, he falls for <p>Gundred</p>, hoping to fake her execution.
