After Japanese spies from the infamous Black Dragon Society infiltrate United States borders, Federal Agent <p>Rex</p> <p>Bennett</p> is enlisted by the government to capture them. As <p>Oyama</p> <p>Haruchi</p>, leader of the dangerous paramilitary group, begins to destabilize the United States war effort through sabotage and corruption, <p>Bennett</p> must team up with British special agent <p>Vivian</p> <p>Marsh</p> and Chinese special agent <p>Chang</p> <p>Sing</p> to stop them and help win the war.
