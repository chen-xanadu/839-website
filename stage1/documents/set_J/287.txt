<p>Paul</p> <p>Cameron</p> is an insurance executive who finds out he has a brain tumor. His family will receive nothing under his current policies, but there is a huge reward for information leading to the arrest of the murderer of a businessman. <p>Cameron</p> frames himself for the murder in the hopes of collecting the reward money for his wife in an anonymous bank account. <p>Cameron</p> is found guilty and sentenced to death, but then is cured of the disease, and escapes in order to find the real killer and clear his name.
