<p>Frank</p> <p>Thompson</p> awakens in the middle of the street after wreckage falling from a building in New York City hits him on the head. <p>Frank</p> soon discovers that his apartment has been rented out for a year and his wife <p>Virginia</p> has been living on her own elsewhere.
<p>Frank</p> confronts <p>Virginia</p>, who is shocked to see the husband who disappeared without explanation a year earlier. As Frank slowly pieces together his old life, it turns out he is running from a murder he cannot remember committing, and <p>Joe</p> <p>Marucci</p>, a detective, is shadowing his every move.
<p>Ruth</p> <p>Dillon</p> introduces herself and takes <p>Frank</p> to the mansion of the wealthy Diedrich family, where she has been employed as a servant. Family matriarch Grandma <p>Diedrich</p> was an eyewitness to the murder of son <p>Harry</p>, but she is a housebound invalid who also is mute. Through sign language, <p>Harry</p> learns from her that <p>Ruth</p> is the killer, <p>Harry</p> having caught her stealing. <p>Harry</p>'s life is in danger as well until <p>Marucci</p> arrives and catches the culprit.
