The setting of the film is the Philippines in 1827, while it was under Spanish rule. <p>Roberto</p> <p>Balagtas</p> (<p>Delgado</p>) is falsely arrested for treason and sent to prison where he is tortured. He escapes with other prisoners, but only <p>Batagtas</p> survives the escape, carrying with him a treasure map left by one of the others. He crosses paths with <p>Ming</p> <p>Tang</p> (<p>Strong</p>) and a group of Chinese smugglers, with whom he finds the treasure. The booty makes him extremely wealthy, and he changes his name to <p>Don Diego</p> <p>Sebastian</p>. He then goes back to the Philippines to seek his revenge.[2]
