The film tells the story of <p>Dorothy</p> <p>Mackaill</p> who comes between <p>Milton</p> <p>Sills</p> and his estranged son. <p>Sills</p> is a carnival barker who is in love with a dancing girl and is ambitious to have his son, <p>Fairbanks</p>, become a lawyer. <p>Fairbanks</p> has other ideas and during his vacation he hops a freight, joins the carnival, and weds <p>Mackaill</p>. Eventually, <p>Fairbanks</p> fulfills the ambition his father had for him.
