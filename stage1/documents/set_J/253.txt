The story tells of vacationing American artist <p>Jeff</p> <p>Farrell</p> who becomes romantically involved with an older woman named <p>Eve</p> <p>Beynat</p>, in southern France, while harboring some attraction for her teenage stepdaughter <p>Annette</p>. <p>Eve</p>'s husband/<p>Annette</p>'s father <p>Georges</p> is in an asylum for, four years ago, using a blowtorch to kill a man who had raped <p>Annette</p>. Believing it will help make <p>Eve</p> his for life, <p>Jeff</p> agrees to assist her in springing <p>Georges</p> from the asylum. Of course, <p>Eve</p> has a completely different agenda in mind. Inspector <p>Etienne</p> sets up a plot to help trap the real killer and the climactic scenes are set at Les Baux-de-Provence in the huge stone galleries dug into the rock of the Val-d'Enfer on the road to Maillane.
