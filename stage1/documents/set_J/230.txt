After finding out her father and his estate is in danger, <p>Princess</p> <p>Emanuella</p> saves his life by marrying <p>Cathos</p>, a grotesque hunchback. She actually is in love with <p>Leonardo</p>, his attractive younger brother. They already had an affair before the marriage, but continue secretly meeting each other. In the end, <p>Cathos</p> finds out about his wife's unfaithfulness and stabs both his wife and brother to death.[1]
