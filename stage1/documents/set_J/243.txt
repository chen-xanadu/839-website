Sea captain <p>Jim</p> <p>Marsden</p> is about to be hanged for a murder he didn't commit, and is rescued from the gallows by two of his crewmen. To clear the captain's name, they head for the island of Pulinan, where they believe the real murderer is hiding. During the search for the killer, one thing leads to another and <p>Jim</p> and the crew soon find that their troubles have just started. Investigating a possible hiding place of the killer, <p>Jim</p> encounters huge sea monsters in Haunted Harbor.
