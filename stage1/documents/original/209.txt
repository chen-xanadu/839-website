The film tells the story of Dorothy Mackaill who comes between Milton Sills and his estranged son. Sills is a carnival barker who is in love with a dancing girl and is ambitious to have his son, Fairbanks, become a lawyer. Fairbanks has other ideas and during his vacation he hops a freight, joins the carnival, and weds Mackaill. Eventually, Fairbanks fulfills the ambition his father had for him.
