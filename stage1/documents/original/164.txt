The unscrupulous Jimmy, runs an agency that searches for heirs of those who have died without leaving a will, and often provides phony claimants in order to collect his fee. When his former girlfriend Joan, who left him to due his lack of ethics, accepts a position at the allegedly legitimate firm owned by Charles, Corrigan investigates Wallingham's background and discovers his rival is even more duplicitous than he is. He exposes Wallingham as a phony and promises to go straight if Joan will come back to him.
