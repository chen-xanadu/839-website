The story revolves around three married couples living in New York City who take vacations together during each of the seasons. After this pattern has been established, <p>Nick</p> leaves his wife of 21 years, <p>Anne</p>, during the Spring trip to the cabin, for a much younger woman, <p>Ginny</p>. He then proceeds to bring <p>Ginny</p> on the Summer, Fall, and Winter vacation trips. This causes the other two couples to be uncomfortable, feeling as if they have betrayed their good friend <p>Anne</p>.
