<p>Tom</p> <p>Martel</p>, a judge's son, returns to town out West after finishing law school. He becomes involved in a personal feud involving a banker's daughter, <p>Hettie</p> <p>Huston</p>, who attempts to railroad poor scrubwoman <p>Mary</p> <p>Shoemaker</p> in the theft of fifty dollars from a local widow.
