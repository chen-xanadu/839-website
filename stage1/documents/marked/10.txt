Set in rural 1840s Scotland, the plot focuses on labor and class issues while telling the story of <p>Gavin</p>, a staid cleric newly assigned to Thrums' Auld Licht church, and <p>Babbie</p>, a member of the nobility who disguises herself as a gypsy girl in order to interact freely with the local villagers and protect them from her guardian, Lord <p>Rintoul</p>, who wants to keep them under his control.
