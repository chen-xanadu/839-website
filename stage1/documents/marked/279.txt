In a desperate attempt to save her faltering marriage, 55-year-old <p>Barbara</p> <p>Sawyer</p> submits to full-body plastic surgery in a Swiss clinic, then checks into an exclusive ski resort Cortina d'Ampezzo to await the arrival of her attorney husband <p>Mark</p>. Reveling in her considerably younger and tauter appearance, she allows playboy <p>Erich</p> to seduce her. When <p>Mark</p> finally arrives, he makes an announcement that changes <p>Barbara</p>'s initial plans forever.
