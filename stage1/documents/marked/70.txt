<p>Nora</p> <p>Shelley</p> is a tax expert for the accounting company which is led by <p>Paul</p> <p>Martin</p>. She thinks she can find a suitable husband by inspecting their clients' tax documents. <p>Martin</p> finds out and tries to dissuade her from this approach, later enlisting the help of his friend <p>Steve</p> <p>Adams</p>, who tries to woo <p>Shelley</p>.
