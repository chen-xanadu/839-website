A feud between the <p>Colby</p> and the <p>Hayden</p> families, starts in the hills of Kentucky and continues in the West after the American Civil War. Also involved is the conflict between vigilantism and the law in a frontier environment, and lovers from the two feuding families. At one point, one of the villains shoots the head off <p>Shirley</p> <p>Temple</p>'s character's doll.
