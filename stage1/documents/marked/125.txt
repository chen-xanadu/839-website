After her family's mansion is burned down by Yankee soldiers for hiding the rebel leader Captain <p>Sam</p> <p>Starr</p> (<p>Randolph</p> <p>Scott</p>), <p>Belle</p> <p>Shirley</p> (<p>Gene</p> <p>Tierney</p>) vows to take revenge. Breaking <p>Starr</p> out of prison, she joins his small guerrilla group for a series of raids on banks and railroads, carpetbaggers and enemy troops. <p>Belle</p>'s bravado during the attacks earns her a reputation among the locals as well as the love of <p>Starr</p> himself. The pair get married, but their relationship starts to break down when <p>Sam</p> <p>Starr</p> lets a couple of psychotic rebels into the gang, leaving <p>Belle</p> to wonder if he really cares about the Southern cause.
