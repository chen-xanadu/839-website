An all-boys orphanage is not far from a prison camp near a swamp. <p>Leslie</p> <p>Henderson</p>, a teen boy, gets to know one of the convicts, <p>Rudy</p> <p>Krist</p>, who saved Les's life from a rattlesnake.
<p>Les</p> stands up for a defenseless boy when bully <p>Tom</p> <p>Bradley</p> makes fun of a boat the boy made. <p>Les</p> is punished at the orphanage by a week of his summer vacation being taken away. Successful businessman Max Cole mocks boys like <p>Les</p> who look out for anybody but themselves. At the prison, <p>Rudy</p> and his inmate friend <p>Doosy</p> are treated inhumanely by a brutal warden, <p>Plug</p>.
<p>Les</p> decides to let <p>Rudy</p> know where the boat is, in case it can help him escape. When he sees <p>Rudy</p> flee in one direction, <p>Doosy</p> runs the other way, but is hunted down by prison dogs and killed. <p>Rudy</p> vows revenge but he, too, ends up dead. <p>Cole</p> feels sorry for <p>Les</p> and offers him a job, but <p>Les</p> has other plans for how to spend the rest of his life.
