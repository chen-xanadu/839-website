<p>Richard</p> <p>Dix</p> is a modern Lothario who has so many sweethearts that his father does not know what to do with him. Tired of paying to get his son out of one romantic entanglement after another, <p>Frank</p> <p>Currier</p> sends his son to the Basque region of France, believing that the women there will only accept attentions from their own people.
