Captain <p>Phillip</p> <p>Donlin</p> (<p>Lloyd</p> <p>Bridges</p>) and his small troop must rush to reach Little Big Horn in order to warn General <p>Custer</p> of the Sioux attack that awaits him. As they race against time, and <p>Donlin</p> pushes them hard through an arduous and dangerous journey, the Sioux start taking out the soldiers one at a time. Meanwhile, <p>Donlin</p> also clashes with Lt. <p>John</p> <p>Haywood</p> (<p>John</p> <p>Ireland</p>), who <p>Donlin</p> knows is having an affair with his wife, <p>Celie</p> (<p>Marie</p> <p>Windsor</p>).
