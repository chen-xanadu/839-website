Housewife <p>Natalie</p> <p>Ravenna</p> (<p>Shirley</p> <p>Knight</p>) decides that she needs a break from marriage after discovering she is pregnant. She meanders across the United States trying to deal with the notion of being responsible. Along the way she meets a man oddly named Killer (<p>James</p> <p>Caan</p>) with a past he is not ready to reveal. It leads <p>Natalie</p> to ask: should she stay with Killer or return to her husband <p>Vinny</p>? Things are further complicated when <p>Natalie</p> gets involved with a handsome but lonely highway patrolman <p>Gordon</p> (<p>Robert</p> <p>Duvall</p>).
