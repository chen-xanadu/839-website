A group of copper miners, Southern veterans, are terrorized by local rebel-haters, led by deputy <p>Lane</p> <p>Travis</p>. The miners ask stage sharpshooter <p>Johnny</p> <p>Carter</p> to help them, under the impression that he is the legendary Colonel <p>Desmond</p>. It seems they're wrong; but <p>Johnny</p>'s show comes to Coppertown and <p>Johnny</p> romances lovely gambler <p>Lisa</p> <p>Roselle</p>, whom the miners believe is at the center of their troubles.
