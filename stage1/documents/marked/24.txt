After being court-martialed and discharged from the Navy, Captain <p>Bell</p> (<p>Rock</p> <p>Hudson</p>) turns to drink. Reduced to skippering a rundown brigantine in the South Seas he takes on board a disparate group of passengers and crew; including a prostitute, a show-biz entrepreneur, a missionary, a washed up opera singer, and a couple of refugees. During a storm at sea, the true characters of all on board are revealed.
