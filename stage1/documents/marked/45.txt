Starting with a wordless jewel heist pulled-off by thief <p>Peter</p> <p>Curran</p> and locksmith <p>John</p> <p>Bain</p>, <p>Curran</p> then double-crosses his accomplice, dumps his lover <p>Gianna</p> and escapes with his ill-gotten gains. In the aftermath <p>Gianna</p> teams up with <p>Bain</p> and the two of them decide to even the score with <p>Curran</p>, developing feelings for each other along the way.[3]
