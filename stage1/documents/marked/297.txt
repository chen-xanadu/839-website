The behavior of <p>Mark</p> <p>Lamphere</p>, an architect, turns strange shortly after his honeymoon with bride <p>Celia</p>, who begins finding out that <p>Mark</p> has many secrets.
It turns out he was married before, his wife died suspiciously and they have a son. He also has a fiercely loyal secretary, Miss <p>Robey</p>, whose face is disfigured.
<p>Mark</p> appears to be somewhat delusional and could be intending to murder <p>Celia</p> inside a room he keeps locked. The disturbed Miss <p>Robey</p> ends up setting fire to the house, whereupon <p>Mark</p> redeems himself in <p>Celia</p>'s eyes by saving her life.
