<p>Wild</p> <p>Bill</p> <p>Hickok</p> is haunted by his dreams of a giant white buffalo. So much that he travels the West to find the beast. Along the way, <p>Hickok</p> meets <p>Crazy</p> <p>Horse</p>, who is also searching the plains for the giant white buffalo, who has killed <p>Crazy</p> <p>Horse</p>'s daughter. <p>Hickok</p> and <p>Crazy</p> <p>Horse</p> team up to kill the elusive buffalo.
