A federal <p>Holt</p> goes undercover to infiltrate a drug smuggling operation headed by a mysterious <p>Van</p> <p>Sloan</p>, a criminal mastermind whose identity is unknown even to his henchmen. <p>Van</p> is also running a bogus hospital where victims are killed on the operating table, and their coffins stuffed with narcotics. The drug-filled coffins are then buried in a cemetery.
