As described in a film magazine,[3] <p>George</p> <p>Travelwell</p>, an American youth motoring in Morocco, discovers that the governor of <p>Campeau</p> has seized a young American woman for his harem. Disguised as an inmate of the harem, <p>George</p> nearly wrecks the place while he rescues her. One thrilling incident follows upon the heels of another in their attempts to get away, and it ends with him setting one tribe against another, leaving them free to peacefully ride away.
