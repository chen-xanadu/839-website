<p>Willie</p> <p>Bauche</p>, a Hollywood producer, becomes so obsessed with turning his wife, <p>Ann</p> <p>Garantier</p>, into the sexiest star in Hollywood that he neglects her real needs. Feeling lonely and tired of Tinseltown, <p>Ann</p> returns to her native France and finds herself attracted to <p>Marco</p> <p>Ranieri</p>, a handsome and very attentive pilot. When <p>Willie</p> hears about the budding affair, he flies into a rage and hires assassins to kill his rival. Unfortunately for him, the killers are romantics and decide that <p>Ann</p> and <p>Marco</p> are so in love that both must die so they can always be together. When <p>Willie</p> finds out, he rushes over to France to try and save his wife.
