U.S. Army private <p>Jerry</p> <p>Walker</p> is in hot water with his sweetheart, <p>Winnie</p> <p>Porter</p>, for putting off their wedding, and with his superiors on the base after crashing a Jeep.
To raise money to pay for the damages and avoid six months of guard duty, <p>Jerry</p> accepts a $300 proposal from three matronly women, <p>Cornelia</p>, <p>Nancy</p> and <p>Maggie</p>, to arrange dates for them with young soldiers. <p>Jerry</p> ropes his pals <p>Barney</p> and <p>Frankie</p> into it, then scrambles when they try to squirm out of it.
<p>Winnie</p>, meantime, figures out <p>Jerry</p> is up to something. She shows up with the girlfriends of <p>Barney</p> and <p>Frankie</p>, after which everybody takes turns trying to make the others jealous. <p>Jerry</p> finally flees, only to end up hooked by a German submarine. He ends up in the brig, but it's a year later and <p>Winnie</p>, now his wife, comes to visit along with their baby.
