In the opening scene set in Southern California, while <p>Virginia</p> <p>Wilson</p> (<p>Anita</p> <p>Ekberg</p>) is taking an outside beach shower, an escaped madman from the sanitarium shows up. He stabs her dog, <p>Rusty</p> [<p>Devil</p> was the name of her second dog], attacks her and is then shot to death by her stepbrother, <p>Charlie</p> (<p>Romney</p> <p>Brent</p>), with a rifle.
After the attack, <p>Virginia</p> is committed to a sanitarium. The psychiatrist falls in love with her. He fakes her death, and they go on the lam. <p>Virginia</p> ends up dancing at the El Madhouse night club run by Gypsy <p>Rose</p> <p>Lee</p>. <p>Lee</p> performs "Put the Blame on Mame," the classic noir theme from the film Gilda.
All the while <p>Virginia</p> is being stalked by a serial killer.
