Police Sgt. <p>Whitey</p> <p>Brandon</p> works for the Vice Squad and is determined to beat corruption in the city. He encounters <p>Carol</p> <p>Hudson</p> who is working as a model. She is sent to frame him and succeeds. <p>Carol</p>'s sister comes to visit and is raped and bashed by a thug who knows <p>Carol</p>. <p>Carol</p>, desperate for revenge, enlists the help of <p>Brandon</p> to fight the thugs who attacked her sister.
