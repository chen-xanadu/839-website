<p>Harry</p> <p>Langdon</p> is a Belgian immigrant to the United States who has fallen in love with <p>Mary</p> <p>Brown</p>, a blind woman. They met as pen-pals when he was fighting in Europe during World War I. <p>Mary</p> even sent <p>Paul</p> a photo of her.
<p>Paul</p> searches for <p>Mary</p> <p>Brown</p> by asking every woman he meets if she is <p>Mary</p> <p>Brown</p>. By accident he rescues her town from crooks and bootleggers.
