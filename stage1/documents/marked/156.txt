<p>Baptista</p> <p>Minola</p> is attempting to marry off his two daughters; however, he will marry off his youngest, <p>Bianca</p> only if someone will marry his eldest, <p>Katharina</p>. <p>Katharina</p> is an ill-tempered shrewish woman but a lusty young nobleman, <p>Petruchio</p>, takes on the challenge of taming and marrying her. A subplot involves the wooing of <p>Bianca</p> by several suitors including handsome <p>Lucentio</p>, foppish <p>Hortensio</p>, and elderly <p>Gremio</p>.
