The film focuses on the courtship and marriage of aging Greek <p>Theo</p> <p>Tomasis</p>, who rose from his humble peasant roots to become an influential mogul who owns oil tankers, airlines, and Mediterranean islands and longs to be elected President of Greece, and considerably younger <p>Liz</p> <p>Cassidy</p>, the beautiful widow of the assassinated President of the United States. The two first meet when she is visiting his island estate with her husband <p>James</p>, the charismatic Senator from the state of Massachusetts. <p>Theo</p> immediately is attracted to her and, despite the fact she obviously is happily married, begins to woo her aboard his yacht while her husband is deep in conversation with the former Prime Minister of the United Kingdom. As the plot unfolds, <p>Theo</p>'s beloved son <p>Nico</p> dies in an accident, his wife <p>Simi</p> commits suicide, <p>James</p> becomes President and appoints his brother <p>John</p> <p>Attorney</p> <p>General</p>, and <p>Theo</p> ends his affair with <p>Paola</p> to comfort and eventually marry grieving widow <p>Liz</p>.
