<p>Naomi</p> <p>Iverson</p> learns that her father has assumed the blame for engaging in an illegal stock pool and is to be sentenced by the Federal Government to 5 years in prison. In return, <p>Iverson</p> will receive a check for $1 million from <p>John</p> <p>Hardin</p>, his former partner and now his bitterest enemy.
