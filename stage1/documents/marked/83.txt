A young boy named <p>Juanito</p> <p>Hernandez</p> befriends and nurses back to health a horse that undergoes an operation. When the horse recovers, owner <p>Clarissa</p> <p>Stewart</p> and her trainer, <p>Charlie</p>, come to believe <p>Juanito</p>'s hunch that the recovered horse now has a good chance to win the Kentucky Derby.
