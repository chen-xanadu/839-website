As described in a film publication,[2] <p>Grizel</p> is the daughter of <p>Taliaferro</p>, who believes that her lover will one day return. <p>Grizel</p> is ostracized by the other children of the town. <p>Hughes</p> and his sister <p>Elspeth</p> come to the town. <p>Tommy</p> is friendly, but <p>Elspeth</p> keeps her distance. When the Painted Lady dies, <p>Greene</p> makes <p>Grizel</p> his housekeeper.
