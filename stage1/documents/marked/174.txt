Private detective <p>Christopher</p> <p>Adams</p> chases a precious antique jade lion through the Mexican cafes, auto courts, and the seamy side of Los Angeles.
<p>Adams</p> has a meeting scheduled with a mystery man named <p>Gomez</p>, who is killed. <p>Alice</p> <p>Ashley</p>, a woman he encounters in a bar, then picks <p>Adams</p>' pocket, stealing his wallet. When he tracks her down, he finds <p>Alice</p> stabbed to death. Nosy neighbor <p>Theresa</p> <p>Appleby</p> happens upon the scene after <p>Adams</p> arrives.
A police lieutenant, <p>Sam</p> <p>Wilson</p>, immediately suspects <p>Adams</p> of murdering the woman. Investigating together, they conclude that a bartender, <p>Patrick</p> <p>Macy</p>, once engaged to <p>Adams</p>, is behind the killings. Mrs. <p>Appleby</p> also helps a grateful <p>Adams</p> in solving the case.
