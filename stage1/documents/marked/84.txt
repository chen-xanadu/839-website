In the contemporary areas of Alaska, <p>Mara</p> <p>Wade</p> (<p>Lori</p> <p>Saunders</p>) is a barefoot wild woman whose parents are killed in a Bear attack. Raised by wolves and wearing only a fur dress, she rescues and befriends an anthropologist named <p>Ken</p> <p>Williams</p> (<p>Adam</p> <p>West</p>) who is interested in teaching her what is in the world. But she is hunted as well by a ruthless carnival worker named <p>Jarnagan</p> (<p>Theodore</p> <p>Marcuse</p>) causing <p>Ken</p> to work to keep her safe.
