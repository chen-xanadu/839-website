After the death of <p>Max</p>'s (<p>John</p> <p>Carradine</p>) wife <p>Lila</p> (<p>Veda</p> <p>Ann</p> <p>Borg</p>), he holds a funeral for her. However, he has also turned her into a zombie. He is amazed when <p>Lila</p> show signs of free will and challenges him for control. In the excitement Dr. <p>Keating</p> (<p>Barry</p> <p>Macollum</p>) goes missing after entering a tomb which should not have been entered.
During dinner, <p>Scott</p> <p>Warrington</p> (<p>Mauritz</p> <p>Hugo</p>) finds a radio in <p>Max</p>'s cabinet, and figures out that it communicates to <p>Hitler</p>. <p>Max</p> learns of this and gags and ties up <p>Scott</p>. <p>Lazarus</p> (<p>James</p> <p>Baskett</p>), <p>Max</p>'s right-hand man, finds a gun. While making soup with <p>Rosella</p> (<p>Sybil</p> <p>Lewis</p>), <p>Jeff</p> (<p>Mantan</p> <p>Moreland</p>) finds <p>Scott</p> bound and gagged in a closet, and he tells <p>Jeff</p> about the situation. <p>Max</p> discovers this and tries to flee the swamp. <p>Lila</p> and the hordes of zombies pursue <p>Max</p>, and both <p>Max</p> and <p>Lila</p> end up sinking into quicksand.
