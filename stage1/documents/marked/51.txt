<p>Pablo</p> <p>Morales</p>, part-owner of the Foster & Morales Wild West Show, is killed during a stunt on horseback. <p>Steve</p> <p>Lacey</p> plans to replace him in management and in the act, so he is irate at learning <p>Rex</p> <p>Allen</p> is going to be the show's new star.
<p>Pablo</p>'s young daughter <p>Juanita</p> takes an immediate liking to <p>Rex</p> and his talented horse, <p>Koko</p>. But when another accident results in a broken leg for <p>Koko</p> that could prove fatal for the horse, <p>Rex</p> and his sidekick <p>Muscles</p> <p>Benton</p> suspect foul play.
<p>Koko</p> recovers thanks to the care of <p>Juanita</p> and her governess, <p>Janet</p> <p>Wells</p>, and <p>Rex</p> feels he should give the horse to the girl. After a fight with <p>Lacey</p>, he intends to leave until he learns that <p>Juanita</p>, now the show's co-owner, has just $2,000 left. After a lab analysis proves the chicanery involved, <p>Foster</p> shoots a sheriff, but <p>Rex</p> manages to make sure the guilty parties both end up behind bars. A grateful <p>Juanita</p> feels that <p>Koko</p>'s rightful place is with <p>Rex</p>.
