<p>Ramona</p> chronicles the romance between <p>Ramona</p>, a Spanish orphan from the prestigious Moreno family, and <p>Alessandro</p>, an Indian who appears on her family's ranch one day. A man named <p>Felipe</p>  proclaims his love for <p>Ramona</p>, but she rejects him because she has fallen for <p>Alessandro</p>. They fall deeply in love, yet their desire to wed is denied by <p>Ramona</p> stepmother, who reacts by exiling <p>Alessandro</p> from her ranch.
