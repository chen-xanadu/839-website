A U.S. cavalry officer, <p>Hemp</p> <p>Brown</p> (<p>Rory</p> <p>Calhoun</p>), runs into some serious trouble when the party of civilians and troops he's bringing to a nearby Army fort is ambushed. A woman is killed during the gunfight, and money is stolen by the bandits. The leader of the gang, <p>Jed</p> <p>Givens</p> (<p>John</p> <p>Larch</p>), is an acquaintance of <p>Brown</p>, who implicates the fugitive during the subsequent trial. But <p>Brown</p> is court-martialed and booted from the Army. <p>Brown</p> tracks down <p>Givens</p> to restore honor to his name.
