When his partner is gunned down, <p>Frank</p> <p>Hovannes</p>, a detective inspector with the New York police department, wants to lead his organized-crime unit against the mob. Legal and departmental restrictions inhibit him, so <p>Hovannes</p> decides to take the matter into his own hands.
A vigilante act, a contract hit against one of the crime syndicate's members, is designed to stir the mob into action so that <p>Hovannes</p> and his men can catch them in the act. He runs into strong objections from his superiors in the police force along the way.
