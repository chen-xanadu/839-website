<p>Peter</p> <p>Potter</p> (<p>Bob</p> <p>Hope</p>) is a dentist of doubtful competence. Out west, after the partner of <p>Calamity</p> <p>Jane</p> (<p>Jane</p> <p>Russell</p>) is killed while trying to discover who's been illegally selling guns to Indians, the cowardly <p>Painless</p> ends up married to <p>Jane</p>, who needs to keep her true identity a secret.
One day while protecting everyone during a hold-up, <p>Jane</p> gives all the credit to <p>Painless</p>, who becomes the townsfolk's "brave" new hero.
