As described in a film magazine review,[3] <p>Dearholt</p> is mysteriously murdered in front of his home, and his beautiful wife <p>Kane</p> vows revenge on the murderer. To this end she becomes a German spy for <p>Karl</p>, <p>MacLean</p>, but the two fall in love. At the outbreak of war <p>Karl</p> is called to Germany, and <p>Liane</p> opens her home to wounded French soldiers. She accidentally learns that <p>Karl</p> is the murderer of her husband and plans to turn him over to the police. Before he leaves, <p>Karl</p> produces letters showing that <p>Sebastian</p> had betrayed his sister, and explains that <p>Sebastian</p> was accidentally shot in a struggle. <p>Liane</p> and <p>Karl</p> escape together on his yacht.
