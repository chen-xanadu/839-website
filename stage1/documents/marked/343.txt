In this police thriller that partly takes place in Chicago Union Station (though filmed instead at Los Angeles Union Station[3]), a railroad policeman, <p>William</p> <p>Calhoun</p>, is approached at work by an apprehensive passenger named <p>Joyce</p> <p>Willecombe</p> (<p>Nancy</p> <p>Olson</p>) who believes that two travelers aboard her train may have been up to no good.
<p>Joyce</p> is the secretary to a rich man named <p>Henry</p> <p>Murchison</p> (<p>Herbert</p> <p>Hayes</p>), whose blind daughter, <p>Lorna</p>, has been kidnapped and held for ransom. The railway station where <p>Calhoun</p> works has been chosen as the location to pay off the ransom. <p>Calhoun</p> and fellow cop Inspector <p>Donnelly</p> race against time to find the kidnappers and bring them to justice.
