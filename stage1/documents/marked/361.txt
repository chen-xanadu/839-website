<p>Andre</p> <p>Laurence</p> (<p>Jourdan</p>) takes a trip to a Polynesian island with his college roommate <p>Tenga</p> (<p>Chandler</p>). He assumes the native life and marries his friend's sister, <p>Kalua</p> (<p>Paget</p>). The island's volcano erupts and the Kahuna, the island's shaman (<p>Maurice</p> <p>Schwartz</p>), decides that the volcano god can only be appeased by the human sacrifice of <p>Kalua</p> to its molten depths. Following her death, <p>Andre</p> says his goodbye to <p>Tenga</p> and returns to civilization.
