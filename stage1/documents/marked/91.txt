<p>Hughes</p> plays <p>Bob</p>, a daredevil aviator in love with <p>Grace</p>. <p>Bob</p>'s reckless behavior and addiction to alcohol causes the death of <p>Grace</p>'s brother, and <p>Bob</p> subsequently loses his job and <p>Grace</p>'s love. <p>Bob</p> puts his life back together and catches a gang of hijackers who were robbing gold shipments from mid-air flights.[2][3]
