In New York in the 1920s, amorous opera star <p>Augustino</p> "<p>Gus</p>" <p>Caraffa</p> (<p>Ezio</p> <p>Pinza</p>) crosses paths with <p>Isabelle</p> <p>Perry</p> (<p>Janet</p> <p>Leigh</p>), a naive music student from Mississippi who is his biggest fan. When a news photographer catches them in a kiss, it is proposed that they get married in name only to avoid a scandal. <p>Isabelle</p>, who is in love with <p>Gus</p>, agrees to the charade, hoping that he will eventually fall in love with her.[2][3]
