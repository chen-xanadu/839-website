As described in a film magazine,[3] <p>Jim</p> <p>Wynnegate</p>, a young Englishman, assumes the guilt for the embezzlement of trust funds that were lost in speculation by his cousin <p>Henry</p>. He embarks to the United States and settles in the west, where he buys a ranch. In a quarrel with <p>Cash</p> <p>Hawkins</p>, <p>Jim</p> is saved from death by <p>Naturich</p>, a young Indian woman, who shoots the outlaw. He marries her out of gratitude and becomes known as the squaw man.
