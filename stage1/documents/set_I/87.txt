The Dead End Kids ride a freight train through California. After the kids get arrested for vagrancy, members <p>Tom</p> and <p>Pig</p> are hired to work on a ranch owned by kindly <p>Mama</p> <p>Posito</p>. <p>Tom</p> learns that <p>Posito</p> hasn't seen her son in years, but believes that he may still be alive. In an attempt to steal her money, <p>Tom</p> decides to pose as her son. However, <p>Posito</p>'s benevolency soon gets the best of <p>Tom</p>, and he decides to stay with her for love, rather than for greed.
