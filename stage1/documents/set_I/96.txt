The film tells the story of a young married couple who become chicken farmers. <p>Betty</p> follows her husband <p>Bob</p> to the countryside where his dream is to be a successful chicken farmer. The problem is, their home is old and needs to be repaired and the baby chicks need constant care. When a rich single woman with a new house and new farm equipment flirts with <p>Bob</p>, <p>Betty</p> questions their decision to move to the farm in the first place. In the end, she finds out that <p>Bob</p> was trying to buy the new house for <p>Betty</p> as a surprise.
