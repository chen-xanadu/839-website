After years of abusing his Austrian immigrant wife <p>Sarah</p> <p>Storm</p>, <p>Jim</p> <p>Grey</p>, a vaudeville entertainer, disappears with their young son Bobby. He eventually sells <p>Bobby</p> to a wealthy couple, the <p>Ashmores</p>. They refuse to return the boy and attempt to return in his stead the deaf mute son of one of their servants.
