After her Chicago gambling hall burns down in the Great Chicago Fire of 1871, <p>Belle</p> <p>Andrews</p> accepts an offer from an ambitious businessmen <p>Harry</p> <p>Farrel</p> to accompany him to Powder River, Montana to open a new casino. However, once there, she becomes disgusted by his criminal activities and joins forces with Wild <p>Bill</p> <p>Hickok</p> to thwart his plans.
