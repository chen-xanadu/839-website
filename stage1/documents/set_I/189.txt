Ensign <p>Ted</p> <p>Jackson</p> (<p>Elvis</p> <p>Presley</p>) is a former U.S. Navy frogman who divides his time between twin careers as a deep sea diver and nightclub singer. <p>Ted</p> discovers what he believes could be a fortune in Spanish gold aboard a sunken ship and sets out to rescue it with the help of go-go dancing yoga expert <p>Jo</p> <p>Symington</p> (<p>Dodie</p> <p>Marshall</p>) and friend <p>Judd</p> <p>Whitman</p> (<p>Pat</p> <p>Harrington</p>, Jr.). <p>Gil</p> <p>Carey</p> (<p>Skip</p> <p>Ward</p>), however, is also after the treasure and uses his girlfriend <p>Dina</p> <p>Bisho</p>p (<p>Pat</p> <p>Priest</p>) to foil <p>Ted</p>'s plans.
<p>Elvis</p> sings six songs in the movie: the title song, "I'll Take Love", "Sing You Children", "You Gotta Stop", "Yoga Is as Yoga Does" in a duet with <p>Elsa</p> <p>Lanchester</p>, and "The Love Machine".
