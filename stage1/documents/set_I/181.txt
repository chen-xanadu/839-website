<p>Mother</p> <p>Bernle</p> is a widow in Bavaria with four sons: <p>Franz</p>, <p>Johann</p>, <p>Andreas</p> and <p>Joseph</p>.
<p>Joseph</p> receives a job offer from the United States, and he is given money to travel there by his mother.
The First World War is heating up. <p>Franz</p>, who is already serving in the German army, is joined by first <p>Johann</p> and then <p>Andreas</p> who is forced into the army.
