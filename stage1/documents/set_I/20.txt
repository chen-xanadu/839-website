Despite the dramatic story elements of child kidnapping, the overall tone of the film mixes comedy and drama. <p>Madeline</p> is a busy and successful actress who is fiercely devoted to her two-year-old son. One day, little <p>Michael</p> disappears from his crib. Miss <p>Fane</p> avoids speaking to the police at first, then calls upon both law enforcement and her legions of fans for help.
