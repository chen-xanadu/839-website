<p>Borden</p> rejects <p>Hamilton</p>, whom she thinks is a chauffeur, in favor of a man she thinks is a millionaire. It transpires that the roles were, in fact, reversed; <p>Hamilton</p> is the millionaire and the other man a chauffeur. <p>Jewel</p> is crushed but manages to do well for herself in business, until she and the real millionaire find themselves reconciled.[2][3]
