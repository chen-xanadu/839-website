Nightclub pianist <p>Sandy</p> <p>Elliott</p> is madly in love with nightclub singer <p>June</p> <p>Mayfield</p>, who ignores his existence, preferring the obnoxious <p>Wally</p> <p>Porter</p>, the nightclub emcee. <p>Sandy</p> follows <p>June</p> to discover to his disgust that she is a big fan of professional wrestling. <p>Sandy</p>'s friend <p>Joe</p> the night club owner decides to make the shy <p>Sandy</p> attractive to <p>June</p> by paying a thug to disrupt <p>June</p>'s singing, then being thrown out by <p>Sandy</p>. <p>Joe</p> adds fuel to <p>June</p>'s new, smoldering love for <p>Sandy</p> by making her promise not to tell a secret: that <p>Sandy</p> is really the masked wrestler known as "The Devil".
