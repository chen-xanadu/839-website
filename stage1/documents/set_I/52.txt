Hellgate Prison is an aptly named facility in the desert where the worst criminals are sent. <p>Hanley</p>, a veterinarian and former Civil War soldier, is falsely accused and convicted of a crime. He is sentenced to this hellish place.
He immediately gets on the wrong side of <p>Voorhees</p>, a vicious guard, and <p>Redfield</p>, a mean convict. <p>Hanley</p> will need to fight his way out, particularly when the prisoners are afflicted with an epidemic of a spreading plague.
