On her way to New York to find financial backing for her impoverished country, the Ruritanian Kingdom of Taronia, Princess "Zizzi" <p>Catterina</p> falls ill with the mumps and has to be quarantined for a month. In desperation, financier <p>Richard</p>, who is planning to issue $50 million in Taronian bonds, hires unemployed lookalike actress <p>Nancy</p> to impersonate the princess, and offers her a large bonus if she changes the mind of the chief opponent of the financial transaction, newspaper publisher <p>Cary</p>.
