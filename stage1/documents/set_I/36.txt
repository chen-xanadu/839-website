The film is set in British East Africa in the early 20th century. Thousands of workers are building the Uganda Railway, Africa's first railroad, and intense heat and sickness make it a formidable task. Two men in charge of the mission are <p>Jack</p> <p>Hayward</p> and Dr. <p>Angus</p> <p>Ross</p>. A pair of man-eating lions are on the loose and completely disrupt the undertaking. <p>Hayward</p> desperately attempts to overcome the situation, but the slaughter continues.
Britain sends three big-game hunters to kill the lions. With them comes <p>Jack</p>'s wife. After the game hunters are killed by the lions, <p>Jack</p> sets out once and for all to kill them. A grim battle between <p>Jack</p> and the lions endangers both <p>Jack</p> and his wife. <p>Jack</p> kills the lions and proves he is not a weakling.
