The Editor of the Daily Clarion newspaper hires amateur criminologist <p>Steve</p> <p>Colt</p> to solve a series of murders, all involving poisonous spider bites.
Meanwhile, King <p>Hitomu</p> has sent his daughter <p>Sombra</p> to the United States to fulfill his plan for global domination. There she poses as a fortune teller and, with a gang of henchmen, attempts to steal a prototype Atomic Rocket Engine.
