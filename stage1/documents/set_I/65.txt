Most of the full plot is unknown. In the version available today, <p>Holmes</p> tells his nephews and children about an adventure he had in the woodlands around Slumber Mountain, near the Valley of Dreams. He finds the cabin belonging to the late hermit <p>O'Brien</p>, who <p>Holmes</p>'s friend <p>Joe</p> once saw carrying a strange telescope-like instrument. That night, <p>Holmes</p> searches the cabin and finds the instrument. Upon doing so, the ghost of <p>O'Brien</p> instructs him to use it to look at the peak of Slumber Mountain.
