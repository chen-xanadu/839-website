Small-town girl <p>Jane</p> falls for <p>Nick</p>, a guy from the big city who offers her the opportunity to get away from her small-town life. He also offers her "headache powder" that she is unaware is really cocaine--and that <p>Nick</p> is a drug dealer. By the time they get to the city, she is hooked on her new medicine. <p>Jane</p>'s brother <p>Eddie</p> goes to the city to look for her, after he and their mother don't hear from her for over a year.
