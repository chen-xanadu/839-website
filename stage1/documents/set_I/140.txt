<p>Brown</p> plays <p>Ossie</p>, a New York City playboy sent to California to straighten himself out. He and his cousin, while driving west, pay too much attention to a sedan full of college girls and rear end a car driven by <p>Pancho</p> <p>Arango</p>. The cousins make it to Hollywood where <p>Brown</p> falls in love with <p>Lugosi</p>'s daughter.
