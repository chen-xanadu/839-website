<p>Charlie</p> <p>Chan</p> (<p>Sidney</p> <p>Toler</p>) is engaged by an heir to solve a mystery on a boat. Miss <p>Nodbury</p> (<p>Ethel</p> <p>Griffies</p>) seeks a pirate treasure on Cocos Island, and the ship she uses is an old sailing ship used in the recent past as a museum of pirate lore by one of her relatives. For safety, she has split her map into four pieces, which she gave to some of the passengers whom she has invited to go along with her, but tells no one who they are. When she is given a fright and succumbs to her heart disease <p>Chan</p> must clear up the mystery while the ship is still at the dock.
