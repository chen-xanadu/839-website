A breakthrough scientific device will revolutionize the world's energy usage if the kidnapped creator can be found. To rescue her father, <p>Claire</p> <p>Forrest</p> enlists the help of private detective, <p>Lance</p> <p>Reardon</p>. Clues lead them to a remote Pacific isle known only as Mystery Island, where the two confront sinister and astonishing forces. The descendants of a long-dead pirate, Captain <p>Mephisto</p> are holding the scientist for their own gain.
