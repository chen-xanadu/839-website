The unscrupulous <p>Jimmy</p>, runs an agency that searches for heirs of those who have died without leaving a will, and often provides phony claimants in order to collect his fee. When his former girlfriend <p>Joan</p>, who left him to due his lack of ethics, accepts a position at the allegedly legitimate firm owned by <p>Charles</p>, <p>Corrigan</p> investigates <p>Wallingham</p>'s background and discovers his rival is even more duplicitous than he is. He exposes <p>Wallingham</p> as a phony and promises to go straight if <p>Joan</p> will come back to him.
