Set in Paris, the story concerns the exploits of wealthy <p>Jack</p> <p>Forbes</p>, who bets his friend <p>Michael</p> <p>Cummings</p> that he can woo and win <p>Looloo</p> <p>Carroll</p> without using any of his money or connections. <p>Cummings</p> hires <p>Simon</p> and <p>Peter</p>, a pair of erstwhile detectives, to make sure that <p>Forbes</p> doesn't win his bet.
